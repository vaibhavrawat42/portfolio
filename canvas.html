Canvas Talent - Talent Agency

VISUAL DESIGN (UI/UX)

This is a design sprint that I did during my internship at The Minimalist. Canvas Talent is an agency that books artists for its clients. The minimalist was approached by Canvas Talent with a project of designing their website. My task during this sprint was to design the a few pages of their website to pitch them our idea and the processes that would follow during the project. I went through a complete process of research, wireframing, designing and documenting during the sprint. Prototyping was left out considering the constraint of time.

Client’s Requirement
They needed a website that can showcase their exclusive artists and their portfolio. They wanted a vibrant look according to the profession they deal with. User, who look for artists for their events should find their desired artists, along with their popularity stats and facilitating the process of exploring events in which they have performed.

Functionality Modules
I structured the site into 7 sections which would serve as the category of navigation.
1. Home Page : Representing the brand and value attributed to the website through graphics and images.
2. Artists Page : Listing different artist provided by the agency with the links to specific pages of each artist.
3. Artist profile page : Contains artists bio, his/ her popularity statistics , gallery and the artist,s requirements.
4. Events : Contains the list of events to be organized by the agency.
5. Clients : Lists of various clients of the agency.
6. Services : services that the agency provides to its clients
7. About : Agency’s description.
8. Contacts : Information including address, contacts and social media links.

Initial Sketches
I started off with paper sketches to decide the initial wireframes of the pages and to visually formulate my ideas. I designed 2 iterations of the landing page . One with the conventional horizontal navigation bar and one with the vertical navigation bar.

Branding
I opted for fonts and color which would be vibrant and elegant at the same time, representative of the agency’s genre of work..
